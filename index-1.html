The arc

Season 1 is the mixtape blast.
Classic rock, party tracks, and the first Evil Morty motif arrive almost immediately.
Season 2 gets existential.
Chaos Chaos, Elliott Smith, and Nine Inch Nails turn jokes into dread and heartbreak.
Later seasons become curatorial.
Hip-hop, 80s pop, jazz, folk, and nostalgic TV themes start functioning like portal coordinates.
